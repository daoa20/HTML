Professional Experience

What we do?

Experience. is a platform which advertises sales and recruitment opportunities for students as well as professionals. We provide CV building advice and online feedback via our ‘contact us’ page. Experience. gives students, especially those who are finishing their degrees, the opportunity to apply for internships [both paid and unpaid], placements, part – time jobs, and full-time jobs.

Our aim

To create job opportunities from different fields of interest on a global scale, while enabling professionals to make connections to increase their productivity and level of success.
